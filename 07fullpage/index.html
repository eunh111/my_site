    about

    Collection
    Samira Boon is a Dutch design studio that makes functional products and textiles with outstanding simplicity and tactility. Drawing inspiration from research into materials and production techniques Samira Boon has created a unique collection of products.

    Collaborations
    Various artists, designers and architects have sought to tap into our insight and design skills, leading to a range of collaborations.

    Commissions
    Our principles are applicable in a wide range of design fields. We are very interested in working with you to create unique solutions and look forward to entering into a dialogue with you.
